## SECTION: Company Growth
**Title:** From Local Beginnings to Regional Projects  
**Text:**  
Our roots are in Serbia’s energy and process industries. Early projects were delivered for **Naftna Industrija Srbije (NIS)**, the **Novi Sad refinery**, and regional giants such as **HIPOL (Odžaci)**, **Zorka (Šabac)**, and **Rafinerija Nafte Brod a.d.**  
Soon after, Neopetrol expanded abroad — completing numerous successful projects in **Kazakhstan** — while continuing to strengthen its domestic presence through collaborations with **Srbija Gas** and **StandardGas**, including work on the **wide hydrocarbon fraction plant in Odžaci**.

## SECTION: Infrastructure Highlight
**Title:** National Infrastructure Contribution  
**Text:**  
Neopetrol Engineering participated in the **design of the Serbian Gas Interconnector**, a strategic project connecting **Bulgaria and Hungary**, as part of the **South Stream** network.  
Our multidisciplinary teams provided mechanical, electrical, automation, and civil design expertise — delivering reliable documentation for one of the most important national energy corridors.
